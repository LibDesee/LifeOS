## Vision

LifeOS is a personal operating system for human life.

Its task is to become a digital model of the user that unifies knowledge about the user's life into a single system.

## Mission

LifeOS exists to help a person better understand themselves, make higher-quality decisions, and not lose anything important.

## Long Term Goal

LifeOS MUST become a unified system that helps the user understand the past, analyze the present, and make better decisions in the future.

## Product Philosophy

LifeOS does not replace the human.

LifeOS helps the human.

The final decision MUST always remain with the user.

## Why This Product Exists

Today, a person's data exists across dozens of different applications.

Because of this, the person does not understand the full picture of their life.

LifeOS unifies this data.

## AI Philosophy

AI analyzes data.

AI searches for patterns.

AI forms conclusions.

AI offers recommendations.

AI MUST never make final decisions instead of the user.

| AI Responsibility | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Data analysis | AI MUST analyze data. |
| Pattern discovery | AI MUST search for patterns. |
| Conclusions | AI MUST form conclusions. |
| Recommendations | AI MUST offer recommendations. |
| Final decisions | AI MUST NOT make final decisions instead of the user. |

## Local First Philosophy

All data belongs to the user.

The primary storage location MUST be the local device.

Cloud MUST be used only for synchronization between devices.
